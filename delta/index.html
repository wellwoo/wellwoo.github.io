Delta

Now on a platform near you

Delta is a passion project by me, Leif Niemczik, open sourced and helped by awesome people. Starting off as a beautification for my homescreen it has since become a top-ranking app in Googles Play Store and was featured in the #myandroid-campaign. With over half a million downloads and 1400+ icons i want to open it up to other platforms like iOS and macOS with the help of the community i built.

So if you feel like helping just head over to GitHub and see if you find something you'd like to add, or just enjoy your new homescreen ☺️

Special thanks to Dani Mahardhika for his awesome Candybar Dashboard that is powering the Android Version of Android.
